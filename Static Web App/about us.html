About Us

If you have stumbled upon our website while looking for a place to buy all things PlayStation, then you have come to the right place! Here at the Playstation Nation,
not only do we have it all when it comes to games, consoles, accessories, and merchandise, but we also have a passionate team of gaming enthusiasts that make up all of our staff.
PlayStation Nation is a gaming store based in Burlington, North Carolina. We have a small, but highly dedicated team of gaming advisors (our front staff), and hardware technicians who work on refurbishing PlayStation consoles. Our affiliation with Sony makes it possible for us to deliver amazing PlayStation products in Burlington.

We are currently growing our team, so if you are interested in joining our team, click here or simply visit our careers page. Apply now to get an opportunity to join the PlayStation Nation team!
