Gaming Advisors needed

Our team is looking to hire gaming advisors to join our team. in this position, you will be a part of a talented team of advisor who will provide exeptional customer service to our customers. You will be working directly with the public and give advise on what games to recommend based on what type of game the customer is looking for. Prior retail experience is not required, but it is preferred.

Gaming Advisor Responsiblilities:
  • Gaming advisors must be welcoming and friendly with all customers when they enter the store and shop.
  • Gaming advisors must also be friendly to other store employees.
  • Must be able to work with a cash register and process sales.
  • Gaming advisors must be fairly knowledgable with PlayStation games, consoles, and other products so that they can make the best recommendation to our customers.
  • Must be able to stand for several hours straight.
  • Perform other tasks as assigned by management.
